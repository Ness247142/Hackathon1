Freedom: another casualty of Covid-19 pandemic


In order to address the COVID-19 outbreak, governments around the world took strict measures and curtailed their citizen’s freedom of movement. Borders of countries, states, provinces, counties and cities all over the world closed. Lock-downs and quarantines obstructed non-essential movement outside of the home. People are required to stay home, to abstain from going to work or school. Restaurants, stores, offices, museums, playgrounds, gyms, etc. also closed. In addition, public gatherings like religious services, concerts, social and sporting events were cancelled.


When will we retrieve our freedom?

“Freedom is not something that anybody can be given. Freedom is something people take, and people are as free as they want to be.”

- James Baldwin
